Billie (born March-ish 2021) is an Australian Cattle Rat (Australian Cattle dog / Rat Terrier mix.)
Not to be confused with a good boy; she's a good gorl! Billie is a domesticated descendent of the wolf.



Early Life

Billie is a rescued street dog from Oklahoma. She was found by a man driving home to Boulder, Colorado on November 18, 2021. She was found without a collar or a chip, but she made herself comfy in the car and insisted on going home with him. Upon her arrival in Boulder, it was clear she wasn’t going to be a good fit with her finder’s dog. This is when her chosen human came into her life, rescued her, named her Billie after Billie Holiday, fashioned her in a Pendleton dog collar, and brought her to her forever home.



Hobbies & Interests

Billie’s DNA encourages herding instincts. In her dream world, she would chase cows by day and snuggle her human all night. She loves herding people, dogs, other animals, and her toys. She loves playing fetch, doin’ a snooze, and zooming around. She is interested in food of all sorts, but especially red meats.



Diet

Billie’s diet consists of Fromm Family Gold dog food, tastefully prepared with a balanced blend of select proteins and wholesome ingredients for adult dogs. Billie indulges in table food when it misses a human’s mouth, her favorite treat. She loves carrots and Dentley dental chews.



Daily Routines

Billie wakes up when her human wakes up, sometimes that’s 6 am and sometimes that’s 11 am. She does poopoos and peepees every day. After eating her breakfast, she does a zoom around the house with her toys for a while. When her human leaves the house, she does some more snoozin’ in her crate. Billie goes on walks, chases balls, goes on a hike with Sunshine Canyon Dog Rescue every Wednesday, eats dinner, cuddles humans, and barks incessantly at any and every sound that exists.



Connections

Billie is connected to many other dogs, even though if it were up to her she would be the only dog in the entire universe. She expresses high interest in cats, but the verdict on how Billie and cats co-exist remains unknown. See also: Harley and Kody, as well as Bella and Jackson!
